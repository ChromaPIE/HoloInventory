[center][size=7]HoloInventory[/size]
[size=5]Adding holograms to Minecraft since 2013![/size]
[b][size=4][url="https://github.com/dries007/HoloInventory"]> My up to date status page, source code & downloads <[/url][/size][/b]
This mod adds a holographic content display to Minecraft. You cant interact with it, its just for show :p

The hologram displays whenever you hover over a block with an inventory.
The background color is optional and fully configurable.
To use this on a server, the server needs to have the mod.
